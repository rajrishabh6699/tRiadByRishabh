Hey!

Welcome to the “tRiad”.

Lots of people have told me that when they are in crisis, playing a game or doing something fun helps them to distract themselves from what's going on.

tRiad is an amalgamation of activities related to health, game and music. It provides you a health monitor which calculates your BMI and help you in visualizing your health goals. The Color Game allows you to play with colors and build your own color sense. And finally The Drum Kit, that allows you to create your own drum beats.

So, give it a go....

And have a good time!
