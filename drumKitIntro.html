The Drum kit

The Drum Kit is made by using HTML5, CSS and JS; jQuery is used for providing the sound when any action is performed. It is a cool interface which lets you create your own drum beats. Just press any of the letter D R U M K I T or hit the tile with a click and you are good to go.

“Music is the beat of a drum that keeps time with our emotions.”
